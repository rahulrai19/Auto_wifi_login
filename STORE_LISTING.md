## Title
WiFi Auto Login Assistant

## Short Summary (Max 132 characters)
Automatically log into captive portal WiFi networks. Save your credentials once and let the extension handle the login screen for you!

## Long Description
Tired of typing in your roll number or username every time you connect to your college, office, or public WiFi network? 

**WiFi Auto Login Assistant** is a lightweight, secure extension that completely automates the captive portal login process. Simply save your WiFi login URL, username, and password into the extension once. 

From then on, whenever you connect to the network and the login page opens, the extension will instantly:
✅ Auto-fill your credentials
✅ Click the "Sign in" button automatically
✅ Wait for the login to succeed, and silently close the tab for you!

**Features:**
- **Universal Support:** Works with standard login forms and dynamically loads on almost any captive portal (including standard university/enterprise networks).
- **100% Secure & Private:** Your credentials are saved securely inside your browser's local storage. They are **never** transmitted to any external servers or third parties.
- **Set It and Forget It:** Configure it once in the sleek popup menu and never worry about logging in manually again.
- **Manual Override:** The popup includes a "Login Directly" button to force the login process if your computer's automatic network detection fails to open the page.

Stop wasting time on WiFi login screens and get connected instantly!

## Edge Store Privacy / Justification Fields

**Single purpose description:**
The sole purpose of this extension is to automate the login process for captive portal WiFi networks. It allows users to securely save their WiFi portal URL and login credentials locally, and then automatically fills and submits the login form whenever they are redirected to that specific network's login page.

**Permission justifications:**
- **storage:** Required to securely save the user's WiFi login URL, username, and password locally on their device so they do not have to enter it manually every time.
- **tabs:** Required to open the captive portal login page in a background tab if the user clicks "Login Directly", and to automatically close the tab once the automated login process is complete.
- **http://*/* and https://*/* (Host Permissions):** Required to detect when the user's browser is intercepted and redirected to their specific captive portal login page on any public or private network, allowing the extension to inject the auto-login script.
