# Privacy Policy for WiFi Auto Login

**Effective Date:** July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "WiFi Auto Login" browser extension ("the Extension") handles your personal information.

## 1. Information We Collect
The Extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device:
- **Login URL:** The web address of your WiFi captive portal.
- **Username / Roll No:** The username required to access the network.
- **Password:** The password required to access the network.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
The information you provide is used **exclusively** for the core functionality of the Extension: automatically filling out and submitting the WiFi login form when you navigate to your designated Login URL. 

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- **Local Storage Only:** All data (including your username and password) is stored locally on your device using the browser's native `chrome.storage.local` API. 
- **No External Transmission:** The Extension **does not** transmit your credentials, browsing history, or any other personal data to any external servers, third-party databases, or analytics services. Your data never leaves your device.

## 4. Third-Party Access
We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties. The Extension operates entirely independently on your device.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the Extension does not collect telemetry or contact information, we recommend checking the store listing page periodically for any updates.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the support tab on the extension's store listing.
